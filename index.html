What do I want to do

I want to achieve haptic and force feedback in VR to enhance the perception of body parts in virtual space.
So, I decided to create a VR mechanical glove that allows people to build blocks with their hands in VR and feel the force feedback when holding the blocks.



How do I achieve it?

Building Blocks Stage

3D Assets: Model and create materials in Blender, create shaders in Unity.
Interaction Code: Implement 3D model additions, deletions, rotation, scaling, proportional changes, and copy-paste using C# code. Implement zooming in and out, and rotation of the view.
Computer Vision: Design and recognize hand gestures within VR to complete the aforementioned operations.

Force Feedback Stage

Communication: Achieve the positioning and interaction of the physical glove within VR.
Data Analysis: Determine which model grip points and gestures correspond to which force limits on the glove.
Arduino: Use flex sensors, motors, and other components to collect gesture data and provide force feedback.
Manufacturing: Design the glove's mechanical structure, use 3D printing and laser cutting to produce the glove.
Agentic AI: An intelligent system that automatically judges the user's gestures and movement intentions, adjusting the virtual blocks' responses (e.g., aligning, rotating, adjusting the best position for the blocks, or displaying assistive tips).
